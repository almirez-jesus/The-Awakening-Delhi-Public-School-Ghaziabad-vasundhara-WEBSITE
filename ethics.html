Ethics

The values behind the code

Ethical design addresses the risks, responsibilities, and relationships that arise as synthetic systems gain power and agency.

Core themes

Trust, fairness, and impact

Ethics is the lens through which we judge whether synthetic thought is beneficial, just, and aligned with human values.

Responsibility

Who is accountable when a system acts? Ethical frameworks help define obligations and governance.

Fairness

Design choices influence whether systems amplify bias or support inclusive outcomes.
