Attention

Focus inside the model

Attention is the mechanism by which a system prioritizes certain signals and memories, allowing it to operate efficiently even in a flood of information.

Core themes

What the model chooses to see

Attention determines which features are amplified and which are suppressed, shaping the system’s immediate priorities and future actions.

Signal weighting

Attention assigns importance scores to information, so the model can focus on the most relevant context.

Context sensitivity

Attention is what lets a system adapt its focus depending on the task and the surrounding cues.

TransformersScaling

Dynamic routing

Attention layers route information dynamically, allowing models to recombine signals efficiently.

MemorySalience

Sequence planning

What the model attends to now influences what it remembers and how it predicts the next step.

Explainable focus

Attention patterns can be analyzed to reveal what a model is prioritizing during reasoning.
