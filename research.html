Research landscape

What the labs are studying

Research institutions are converging on a shared question: whether cognition can be made legible enough that its structure, limitations, and possible self-modification become measurable and designable.

Institutions
OpenAI — Large-scale language systems and alignment research.
DeepMind — Agentic systems, world models, and embodied learning.
Anthropic — Safety, interpretability, and frontier model behavior.
MITNeuroscience

Computational neuroscience

Bridging models of neural function with the structure of modern learning systems.

StanfordLLM

Generalization and reasoning

Examining how high-capacity systems generalize across tasks and domains.

OxfordPhilosophy

Ethics and agency

Investigating moral reasoning, autonomy, and the social meaning of artificial agency.
